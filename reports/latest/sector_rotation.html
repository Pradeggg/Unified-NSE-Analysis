Methodology
How each score and screen is calculated.
R
Sector Rotation Score

Ranks sectors by short-term momentum and relative strength versus Nifty 500 benchmark.

0.35 × RS_1M + 0.25 × RET_1M
+ 0.20 × RS_5D + 0.10 × RS_3M
+ 0.10 × RS_6M
S
Investment Score

Composite score combining technicals, relative strength, fundamentals, and pattern/signal bonuses.

0.38 × Tech + 0.27 × RS_rank + 0.25 × Fund
+ Pattern bonus + Supertrend bonus + Signal bonus
T
Supertrend

ATR-based trend indicator using NSE OHLC data. Bullish when price is above the dynamic level, bearish below.

Period: 10 · Multiplier: 3.0
Source: NSE stock OHLC cache
B
Consolidation Breakout

Latest close above prior 20-session resistance, base width ≤ 12%, with volume confirmation ≥ 1.4× average.

Lookback: 20 sessions
Width threshold: 12%
Volume: 1.4× average
P
Peak Resilience

Filters stocks ≤ 20% from 52W high and within 5% of peak, then ranks by tech strength, RS, and recovery velocity.

0.25 Tech + 0.20 RS + 0.20 RecoverySpeed
+ 0.20 HighProximity + 0.15 Fund
