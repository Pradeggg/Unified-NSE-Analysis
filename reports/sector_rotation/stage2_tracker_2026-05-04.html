📖 How to Read This Report

🏗 Weinstein Stage Method

Stage 1 – Basing
Price moves sideways, SMA50 flattening. Stock consolidating after decline. Watch for breakout.

Stage 2 ✅ – Advancing
Price above SMA50 & SMA200, both rising. Best risk/reward for new positions.

Stage 3 – Topping
Price stalls near highs, SMA50 flattening. Tighten stops or exit.

Stage 4 ❌ – Declining
Price below SMA50 & SMA200, both falling. Avoid.

📊 RSI (Relative Strength Index)

< 30 — Oversold (potential reversal up)

30–50 — Weak / recovering

50–70 ✅ — Bullish momentum

> 70 — Overbought (watch for pullback)

📈 Relative Strength (RS)

Price performance vs Nifty 500 over 12 months. Positive RS = outperforming the index.

⚡ Supertrend (ATR-based)

Period = 10, Multiplier = 3. Uses Average True Range (ATR) to set dynamic support/resistance bands.

↑ BULLISH — Price above supertrend band (green support). Trend is up — trailing stop holds.

↓ BEARISH — Price below supertrend band (red resistance). Trend has reversed down.

📡 Trend Signal

STRONG_BULLISH — Both SMA50 & SMA200 rising, price above both.

BULLISH — Price above SMA50.

NEUTRAL — Mixed signals.

BEARISH — Price below SMA50 and/or SMA200.

🔬 Technical Score (0–100)
