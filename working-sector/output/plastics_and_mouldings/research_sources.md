### Research summary (sources and dates)

The India plastic industry is projected to grow significantly, reaching USD 44.28 billion in 2025 with a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 6.30%, and further to USD 60.11 billion by 2030 (Mordor Intelligence, 2026-03-09). The Indian Plastic Molding Market is also expected to grow from USD 45.13 million in 2024 to USD 53.64 million by 2030 at a CAGR of 3.1% (Mordor Intelligence, 2026-03-09; Research and Markets, 2026-03-09). The plastics injection molding market is anticipated to reach 157.13 million tons in 2025 and grow to 193.76 million tons by 2030, driven by various manufacturers including ALPLA, Amcor PLC, AptarGroup, Inc., Magna International Inc., and Silgan Holdings Inc. (Mordor Intelligence, 2026-03-09). The Indian plastics market is valued at USD 46.77 billion in 2025 and projected to reach USD 79.99 billion by 2034, growing at a CAGR of 6.14% from 2026 to 2034 (IMARC Group, 2026-03-09). Additionally, the injection moulders market in India is expected to grow from USD 344.6 million in 2025 to USD 584.4 million by 2035 with a CAGR of 5.3% (Future Market Insights, 2026-03-09).
