## 1. Research question (one sentence)
**What are the key growth opportunities and challenges for India's textile industry in 2026, particularly focusing on smart textiles for military applications, linen cloth market trends, and the impact of strategic factors like the EU-India FTA?**

## 2. Definition
- **Industry term**: Textile Manufacturing (India)
- **Universe rule**: NSE-listed companies involved in textile manufacturing across various segments including apparel, home textiles, technical textiles.
- **Source**: Aligned with industry bodies such as IBEF and the Ministry of Textiles, Government of India.

## 3. Hypotheses
### H1 (Sector)
- **One sentence**: The textile sector will experience significant growth in 2026 due to strategic factors like the potential EU-India FTA, low MOQs, and advancements in smart textiles.
- **How we test it**: Analyze the correlation between these strategic factors and historical performance of similar sectors globally.

### H2 (Stock selection)
- **One sentence**: Stocks that exhibit strong fundamentals, momentum, and are aligned with emerging trends such as smart textiles for military applications will outperform the broader textile index over the next 12 months.
- **How we test**: Implement a screen-based approach using fundamental scores >70, momentum/RSI vs Nifty 500 >0 over the past year.

## 4. Scope and limits
- **In scope**: Listed companies in India involved in textile manufacturing, including those specializing in smart textiles for military applications.
- **Out of scope**: Non-listed manufacturers, private sector entities not publicly traded on Indian exchanges.
- **Known limits**: The analysis is limited to the point-in-time of March 9, 2026 and may be subject to changes due to geopolitical or economic shifts.

## 5. Screen criteria (operational)
- **Quality**: Fundamental score >70
- **Momentum/RSI**: RSI vs Nifty 500 >0 over the past year
- **Composite weights**: Combined scores from quality and momentum metrics
- **Shortlist size**: Top 15 stocks meeting all criteria
