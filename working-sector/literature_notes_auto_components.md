## Source 1

- **Title / Report name:** Indian Auto Component Sector – FY25 turnover and growth (industry coverage).
- **Publisher / Author:** ACMA (Automotive Component Manufacturers Association of India); reported via Motorindia, Modern Manufacturing India.
- **Date / Year:** FY2024–25 (FY25).
- **URL or file:**  
  - https://www.motorindiaonline.in/indian-auto-component-sector-nearly-doubles-in-5-years-hits-₹6-73-lakh-crore-turnover-in-fy25/  
  - https://www.mmindia.co.in/article/2935/acma-auto-components-sector-grows-14-percent-cagr-to-reach-us-80-2-billion-in-fy25
- **Definition of sector:** Auto components = parts and systems supplied to vehicle manufacturers (OEMs). ACMA represents component manufacturers; SIAM represents vehicle manufacturers. OEM supply, aftermarket, and exports are distinct segments.
- **Market size:** ₹6.73 lakh crore (USD 80.2 billion) in FY25; ~9.6% YoY growth. Industry nearly doubled in 5 years; 14% CAGR.
- **Number of players / companies:** ACMA has 1,054+ members; industry reports refer to “organized” and “unorganized” segments. “830+ organized companies” (from initial note) is in line with a broad organized-sector count.
- **Key segments:** OEM sales (₹5.70 L Cr, 10% YoY), Aftermarket (₹99,948 Cr, 6%), Exports (USD 22.9 B). Segments: engine parts, transmission, electrical, tyres, forgings, etc.
- **Key drivers:** Domestic OEM demand, EV transition, PLI schemes, export growth, raw material and supply chain.
- **How our universe aligns or deviates:** We use only **listed** component suppliers (ex-OEM); ACMA covers all components including unlisted. Definition (components vs OEM) aligned.

## Source 2

- **Publisher / Author:** ACMA; Brickwork Ratings / industry summaries.
- **Date / Year:** FY 2023–24 / 2024.
- **URL or file:**  
  - https://www.acma.in/about-us.php/auto-component.php  
  - Industry reports (e.g. Brickwork Ratings – Indian Auto Component Manufacturing Industry).

- **Market size / players:** FY24 ~₹6.2–6.5 L Cr equivalent; 400+ major players (80% share); unorganized for rest.
- **Notes:** Confirms OEM vs component split. Our “₹6.2 Lakh Cr” from the initial handwritten note is consistent with FY24-type figures; FY25 at ₹6.73 L Cr is updated.

## One-paragraph summary for sector note

*Draft for “Definition and market size” in the final report:*

Auto Components in India refers to parts and systems supplied to vehicle manufacturers (OEMs), as defined by ACMA (Automotive Component Manufacturers Association of India). The industry reached a turnover of **₹6.73 lakh crore (USD 80.2 billion)** in FY2024–25, with 9.6% YoY growth and a 14% CAGR over the prior five years (ACMA, FY25). Segments include OEM supply, aftermarket, and exports. Our universe includes only **listed** component suppliers (ancillaries), excluding OEMs (passenger vehicle, 2W, and commercial vehicle manufacturers), and aligns with this definition.
